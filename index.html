Learn RedBook (Xiaohongshu)

RedBook, also known as Xiaohongshu, is a social media and e-commerce app popular for sharing lifestyle tips, product reviews, and more. Here's how to get started:

  • Step 1: Download the app from the App Store or Google Play.
  • Step 2: Create an account using your email or phone number.
  • Step 3: Explore trending topics, follow creators, and engage with content.
  • Step 4: Post your own content to share your tips and reviews!

Master TikTok

TikTok is a video-sharing platform where users create short videos with music, effects, and filters. Here's how to make the most of it:

  • Step 1: Set up your profile and start exploring content.
  • Step 2: Use trending hashtags to discover popular videos.
  • Step 3: Learn editing tools to create engaging content.
  • Step 4: Interact with the community through comments and collaborations.
